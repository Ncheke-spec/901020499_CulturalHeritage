Traditional Basotho Music & Dance

Explore the traditional Basotho music and dance.

Traditional Dances

Mokhibo

This is a dance perfomed by wowmen or girls on their knees moving their shoulders, especially on big events just for entertainment.

Litolobonya

This is also a dance performed by the ladies wearing a "Thethana" that has beer lid on it for a perfect sound.

Mohobelo

This one is performed by gentlemen, mostly older. Other men sing a song called "Mokorotlo", then the ones on the stage move their feet in upwards direction all at the same time

Liphotha

This dance is usually performed by men but can still do too. It is usually for fun because it's very funny. The performers wear gumboots and beat them to make a similar beat or rythm also doing crazy things
